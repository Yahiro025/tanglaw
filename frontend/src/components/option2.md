1. If 3x - 7 = 20, what is the value of x?
a. 7
b. 8
c. 9
d. 10
Answer: c.
Explanation: Add 7 to both sides (3x = 27) Then divide both sides by 3 (x = 9)
2. What is the equation of the line passing through (1,1) and parallel to y = 2x - 5?
a. y = 2x  
b. y = 2x - 1  
c. y = 2x + 1 
d. y = -1/2x + 1
Answer: b.
Explanation: Parallel lines have the same slope. Since y = 2x - 5 has a slope of 2, use m = 2 and the point (1,1). Substituting the point gives b = -1, so the equation is y = 2x-1.
