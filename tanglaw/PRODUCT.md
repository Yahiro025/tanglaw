# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Students seeking scholarship opportunities. These users are often navigating complex eligibility requirements and deadlines, feeling a mix of hope and pressure. They need a tool that simplifies the search and provides clear, actionable steps.

## Product Purpose
TANGLAW is an AI-powered scholarship navigation portal designed to illuminate the path to financial aid. It centralizes scholarship discovery, offers readiness assessments, and provides an AI companion to guide users through the entire application journey.

## Brand Personality
Guiding, Reliable, Professional. The interface should feel like a trusted mentor—knowledgeable and steady, yet approachable and encouraging.

## Anti-references
Static or dull "government-style" portals that feel bureaucratic and cold. "Vibe-coded" designs that prioritize trendy aesthetics over functional clarity. Cluttered layouts that increase cognitive load.

## Design Principles
- **Guiding Light**: The UI should always indicate where the user is and where they should go next, reducing the "where do I start?" anxiety.
- **Empathetic Reliability**: Data-heavy information (deadlines, eligibility) must be presented with absolute precision and trust, but wrapped in a supportive, human-centric tone.
- **Dynamic Utility**: The portal is a tool first, but it shouldn't feel mechanical. Sub-animations, thoughtful transitions, and responsive feedback should make the experience feel "alive" and reactive to the user's progress.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- **High Contrast**: Ensure scholarship details and eligibility text meet WCAG AA standards for readability.
- **Reduced Motion Support**: Provide meaningful alternatives for animations to ensure a comfortable experience for all users.
- **Clear Hierarchy**: Use distinct typographic scales and spacing to assist users with cognitive load or reading difficulties.
